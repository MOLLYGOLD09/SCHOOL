Junior High School

Grades 7 to 10 Comprehensive Education Program

Program Overview

The Junior High School (JHS) is designed to provide students in Grades 7 to 10 with a comprehensive and holistic education. It is part of the K-12 educational system implemented to enhance the country's education quality. The Junior High School (JHS) seeks to provide a well-rounded education that equips students with the knowledge, skills, and values needed for their future academic and career pursuits.

Grade Levels

Explore our comprehensive curriculum for each grade level

Junior High School 7

The Grade 7 JHS Curriculum Program is a comprehensive educational program designed to provide students with a solid foundation in various subject areas while fostering critical thinking, creativity, and a sense of national identity. This program is aligned with the K to 12 Basic Education Program, which aims to equip learners with the knowledge, skills, and values they need to succeed in the 21st century.

Junior High School 8

The Grade 8 JHS Curriculum Program builds upon the foundational knowledge and skills acquired in Grade 7. It continues to align with the K to 12 Basic Education Program, which aims to provide students with a well-rounded education that prepares them for the challenges of the 21st century. This program emphasizes critical thinking, creativity, and values formation while delving deeper into subject-specific content.

Junior High School 9

The Grade 9 JHS Curriculum Program is a pivotal stage in the K to 12 Basic Education Program, designed to build upon the knowledge and skills acquired in the earlier grades while preparing students for more specialized and advanced learning. This program continues to prioritize critical thinking, creativity, and values education while introducing more specialized subjects to cater to students' evolving interests and career aspirations.

Junior High School 10

The Grade 10 JHS Curriculum Program represents a crucial phase in the K to 12 Basic Education Program. It serves as a bridge between the foundational learning in earlier grades and the specialized tracks available in Grades 11 and 12, allowing students to explore their interests and aspirations more deeply. This program maintains a focus on critical thinking, creativity, and values formation while offering students opportunities to further shape their educational path.
