Senior High School

Grades 11 to 12 Specialized Education Tracks

Program Overview

At Silangan National High School, the Senior High School program offers the following strands: Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM) Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS) Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) Home Economics (HE) Industrial Arts (IA) Information and Communication Technology (ICT) These strands are designed to equip students with the necessary knowledge, skills, and competencies to prepare them for higher education, employment, entrepreneurship, and lifelong learning.

Senior High School Strands

Explore our specialized tracks and strands

Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS)

This strand focuses on the study of human behavior and societal changes, and analysis of arts, culture, literature, and politics.

Accountancy, Business and Management (ABM)

This strand would focus on the basic concepts of financial management, business management, corporate operations, and all things that are accounted for.

Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM)

The STEM strand is intended for students who are inclined toward science, mathematics, technology, and engineering. It develops analytical thinking, problem-solving skills, and scientific inquiry, preparing students for science- and technology-related college programs.

Home Economics (HE)

The Home Economics (HE) specialization equips students with skills related to daily living, food preparation, hospitality, and basic entrepreneurship. It prepares learners for careers in service-oriented industries and small business management.

Industrial Arts (IA)

The Industrial Arts (IA) specialization focuses on technical and mechanical skills used in construction, maintenance, and repair works. This specialization is ideal for students who enjoy hands-on work and technical problem-solving.
